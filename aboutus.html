Our Story

The IHave2Watch journey began with three friends who shared an unwavering passion for films and series. They each had their own unique experiences and perspectives on what made a film or television production truly special. However, they faced a common challenge: the difficulty of finding a platform where they could meaningfully share their opinions and discover new titles based on recommendations from people with similar tastes.

Determined to fill this gap, these friends decided to join forces and create something extraordinary. After many nights of brainstorming and heated discussions, the concept of IHave2Watch was born: a space where film and series lovers from all over the world could connect, share their opinions and discover new content collaboratively.

With tireless dedication, they turned this vision into reality. IHave2Watch was launched as a film and series review platform, where users could create personalized profiles, rate productions and write detailed reviews. The app is designed to not only provide a review platform but also to encourage constructive discussions and meaningful connections between community members.

IHave2Watch is not just a list building and sharing platform, but a vibrant and diverse community of cinephiles and series fans from around the world. Our story is a testament to the power of passion and collaboration, and we're excited to continue this journey alongside all of you, sharing our love for the world of entertainment, one film and series at a time.
